# Roadmap

## Today: Public Slice

- Laptop-first playable game.
- Five complete NPCs.
- Memory and gossip.
- Tactical crises.
- Crafting and resources.
- Score, badges, three endings.
- Landing page and public deploy path.

## Phase 2: Online Backbone

- Supabase or Firebase auth.
- Cloud save sync.
- Real online leaderboard.
- Feedback/community table.
- Admin-controlled seasonal crisis seeds.

## Phase 3: Deeper Simulation

- More NPCs.
- NPC-to-NPC autonomous schedules.
- Factions and political assemblies.
- Consequence graph.
- Richer relationship memory.

## Phase 4: Multiplayer

- Asynchronous rival districts.
- Community seasons.
- Shared events.
- Cooperative defense.

## Phase 5: Packaged Releases

- PWA polish.
- Optional Capacitor mobile builds.
- App Store and Play Store packaging.
- Push notifications for seasonal events.
